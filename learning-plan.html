What is your long-term goal and/or career pathway?

  • My long-term goal is to be able to work remotely as a front-end or full-stack web developer.
  • My short-term goal is to freelance for smaller projects and earn while I continue to learn.

A description of your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning

Strengths:

  • I can read pretty quickly and learn/get through content relatively quickly.
  • Discipline > Motivation.
  • I'm good at compartmentalizing things.

Limitations:

  • A mistake I can make is waiting until I feel ready to start.
  • I can spend too much time on things that have less of an impact in terms of long-term goals, so I constantly need to evaluate where my time is best spent, yet also trust that I might not always be right - trust the process.

Working through Challenges

  • Doing more research
  • Linking in with a more knowledgeable-other
  • Enrolling in a course to help fill a gap
  • Investing time to learn the basics/foundations so I can build from there.
  • Asking other people for help
  • Take notes, ask questions
  • Create a Plan for how I will tackle the challenges
  • Keep myself accountable by creating an agenda with a due date where I will give myself the time needed to accomplish the goal.
  • Check back on my goal at the end of the week and check in on my progress.

What to do when things might build up and get to me?

  • Before I start the workday, I read fiction or anything interesting while I have breakfast and then enjoy a hot espresso. I find this relaxes me and it's a nice start to the day.
  • It's non-negotiable to get outside (during lockdown) every day. Even if it's just for a walk, I need it. Recently, I'm either doing yoga or I walk or run.
  • I find I've been dreaming of the terminal and solving problems in my sleep, so I am valuing my sleep and downtime!

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

  • I'd like to continue to grow my relational self-awareness/EQ and read more books on psychology. i.e., Dabrowski's theory on Positive Disintegration.

Expectations of the facilitation team

  • To be there when I have a problem and to suggest how I can come to solutions.
  • I don't need someone to instantly reply to my messages, just at some point. So far, it's all good!
